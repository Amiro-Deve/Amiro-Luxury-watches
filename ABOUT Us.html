✦ Our Story

Born from a Love of Time

Amiro was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate horologists who believed that a watch is more than just a timekeeper — it's a statement, a memory, a legacy.

Every piece in our collection is hand-selected for quality, precision, and beauty. We work with the world's finest craftsmen to bring you timepieces that are as enduring as the moments they mark.

From the ateliers of Switzerland to the workshops of Japan, Amiro sources only the finest movements and materials — because you deserve nothing less than perfection.

What We Stand For

Three principles guide everything we do at Amiro.

⚙️

Precision

Every movement is tested to the highest standards. We believe accuracy is the foundation of trust — in a watch and in a brand.

💎

Craftsmanship

From the case to the clasp, every detail is considered. We collaborate with master artisans who share our obsession with perfection.

🌍

Integrity

Honest pricing, authentic products, real relationships. We're building a brand you can trust — today, tomorrow, and for generations to come.
