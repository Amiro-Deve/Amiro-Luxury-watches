Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Amiro watches, orders, and after-care.

Orders & Shipping
How long does delivery take?
+
Standard delivery takes 5–10 business days worldwide. Express options (2–3 days) are available at checkout for most countries.
Do you offer free shipping?
+
Yes! All Amiro orders include free worldwide standard shipping. No minimum order required.
Can I track my order?
+
Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email so you can follow your timepiece every step of the way.
Returns & Warranty
What is your return policy?
+
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact us and we'll arrange a return at no cost to you.
What does the 1-year warranty cover?
+
Our warranty covers all manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for 12 months from the date of purchase. Normal wear and accidental damage are not covered.
How do I claim my warranty?
+
Contact our support team with your order number and a description of the issue. We'll guide you through the process quickly and efficiently.
Products & Authenticity
Are all Amiro watches authentic?
+
Yes, 100%. Every watch we sell is genuine and comes with its original documentation. We have zero tolerance for counterfeit products.
Do watches come in original packaging?
+
All watches arrive in their original brand packaging, plus our signature Amiro gift box — perfect for gifting or personal keepsake.
Can I request a specific watch not listed?
+
We love special requests! Contact us with the model you're looking for and our team will do their best to source it for you.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
